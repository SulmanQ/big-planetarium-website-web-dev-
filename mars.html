Mars

Mars is a rocky planet known as the Red Planet because of iron rich dust.

Mars has deserts, valleys, and polar ice caps. Scientists explore it using orbiters and rovers.

Quick facts

  • Type: Rocky planet
  • Moons: Phobos and Deimos
  • Day length: About 24.6 hours
  • Famous feature: Olympus Mons giant volcano

Why people care about Mars

Mars may have had liquid water in the past. Studying it helps us understand how planets change over time, and it may help future human exploration.
