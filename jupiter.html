Jupiter

Jupiter is the largest planet in our Solar System. It is a gas giant with strong storms.

Jupiter’s Great Red Spot is a giant storm that has lasted for a long time.

Quick facts

  • Type: Gas giant
  • Day length: About 10 hours
  • Many moons including Io Europa Ganymede and Callisto
  • Famous feature: Great Red Spot

Moons you should know

Some moons of Jupiter may have oceans under ice. This makes them interesting for future exploration.
