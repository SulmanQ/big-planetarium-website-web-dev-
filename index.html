The Milky Way is our home galaxy filled with stars planets and nebulae.

What is the Milky Way?

The Milky Way is a huge spiral galaxy. Our Solar System sits inside one of its spiral arms. When you look up at a dark night sky the Milky Way can appear like a glowing band of light.

Spiral arms

Spiral arms contain gas and dust where new stars can form.

Our location

Earth orbits the Sun and the Sun orbits the center of the galaxy.

Why it's exciting

Studying space helps us understand planets stars and our origins.
